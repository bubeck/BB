  • The profile editor consist of two graphs. The first one shows frequency (in color gradient) and duration of the segments.
  • Second graph shows actual frequency for the selected segment.
  • Click on the segment on the first graph to edit its frequency in the second graph.
  • Tone preview on the right, displays segment frequency on the left and the duration on the right for the actual vario value.
  • Use mouse to grag the points on the graphs to adjust duration, frequency and vario value for the segment
  • When point is selected, you can use mouse wheel and arrows to fine adjust the point position:
    • mouse wheel+/mouse wheel- fine adjust duration or frequency
    • / fine adjust duration or frequency
    • / fine adjust vario value
    • shift locks vario value when changing duration or frequency
    • ctrl locks duration and frequency when changing vario value
    • mouse double click add or remove adjust point on the graph
  • Select Play sound to listen to the tone from the Strato
